How to Play PICK 2

Visit your PA Lottery retailer and pick up a PICK 2 playslip or buy online now.
 
Play up to five panels on your PICK 2 playslip. On each game panel, pick any two-digit number. Or, select the Quick Pick option to have your numbers randomly selected by the computer. View Sample Playslip
 
Pick your play type: Straight, Box, Straight/Box, Super Straight, Front Digit, Back Digit. (Learn more about PICK 2 play types here.)
 
Choose the drawing(s) you wish to play. You can play day, evening, or both drawings.
 
You may play PICK 2 for up to seven draws in advance.
 
If desired, select the "NEW" Wild Ball option. To learn more about how it works, click here.
 
Be sure to check your PICK 2 ticket to verify that the information is correct and legible. View Sample Ticket
 
Look for PA Lottery results: You can check PICK 2 Lottery results by watching the PICK 2 day drawing on the PA Lottery website daily after 1:35 p.m., watching the PICK 2 evening drawing nightly at 6:59 p.m. on TV or streaming online, checking our website and official mobile app, and subscribing to Lottery RSS feeds. You can also visit the Lottery VIP Players Club to sign up for emails and text messages to receive winning Lottery number alerts for PICK 2 and other PA Lottery games.
 

Note: Sales end for the Day drawing at 1:05 p.m. and for the Evening drawing at 6:57 p.m. daily.
